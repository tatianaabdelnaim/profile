Hello, a bit about me:

I recently completed my fifth year and will be on the academic job market in Fall 2026. I am working under the supervision of Dr. Peter Patzt . I received my B.S. in Mathematics from the Lebanese University and my M.S. from the American University of Beirut.

Research Interests

  • Steinberg module
  • Homological Stability

Research Papers
